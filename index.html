Artificial intelligence has seen a rise in popularity, over the past few years, due to the development of new architectures and increased availability of data. This posts are the work of a machine learning student with the strong intention of understanding the mathematics behind the Artificial Intelligence field.
With this I only mean to say that these are mostly subjective opinions and in being so, are probably mistaken in some ways. So, in an attempt to learn from my mistakes it is my wish, for anyone who can comment about my posts, to point out this flaws or simply to give further information or personal thoughts. So, while keeping this in mind, lets start.
